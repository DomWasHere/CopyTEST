Course Workload Calculator

Welcome to the Course Workload Calculator for Tulane University

Purpose

This website is for entering information about the work that is expected to be completed by students
in online courses in order to calculate the total number of hours students can expect to spend on the
course. Target totals are provided so instructors may see immediately how their coursework aligns to
the desired time targets. Capturing this data ensures that each online course offers an equivalent
workload to in-person classes; so, students can feel confident that they are getting a similar experience
regardless of the instruction method of the class they take.

Instructions

  1. Click on the "Enter New Course" button if you are entering a course for the first time.
  2. Click on the "Search" button if you want to return to a course that was already entered.
  3. You can return to the Home screen at any time by clicking on the Home link at the top of the page.
